# Asteriod_Game
A turret-based arcade shooter built in Godot using GDScript, inspired by classic space shooter games. Defend your base against waves of meteors and challenging boss meteors while upgrading your turrets and collecting power-ups.


Features

Turret Upgrade System – Improve firepower and defenses.

Special Effects – Black hole detonations and advanced explosion animations.

Score & Leaderboard System – Track your high scores and compete for the top rank.

Health & Shield Power-ups – Protect your base during intense waves.

Boss Meteors – Face off against massive and challenging meteors.

Custom Turret Skins & Themes – Change the look of your defenses.

Sound Effects & Music – Immersive gameplay experience.

Builds

Windows .exe and Android .apk builds included via Git LFS.
